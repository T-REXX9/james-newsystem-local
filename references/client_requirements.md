# Client Requirements – Daily Call & CRM Module

## 1. Daily Call Requirements

## 1.1 Customer Lists //ok na cheked
Display assigned customers grouped by status with last activity/found dates:
- **Active customers** – e.g., “Active – Nov 1, 2025”
- **Inactive customers** – e.g., “Inactive – July 5, 2025”
- **Prospective customers** – e.g., “Prospective – Aug 27, 2025”

---

## 2. Client Contact & Sales Report View //checked
For each customer:
- Full **contact details**
- Sales report / sales history
- Chat history with **date & time**

---

## 3. Sales Call / Text Log
Record for each call/text:
- Call or text details
- Duration
- Notes about discussion
- Outcome:
  - **Follow‑up**
  - **Negative**
  - **Positive**
  - **Others**

---

## 4. Lead Opportunity Management
System automatically lists:
- Active customers who **did not purchase this month**
- Inactive customers with **positive interest** needing follow‑up
- Prospective customers who are **positive** and need follow‑up

---

## 5. New Customers & Prospectives
Show daily:
- Newly found **prospective customers**
- Newly found **customers**

---

## 6. Customer Summary (Implied)
Customer summary section with aggregated details.

---

## 7. Inquiry & Purchase History
For each customer:
- Inquiry list
- Purchase history
All visible inside the daily call view.

---

## 8. Management Tools
Management can:
- Adjust pricing
- Reassign clients
- Blacklist clients
- Reply to sales reports

---

## 9. Agent Performance Dashboard
Show:
- Monthly quota
- Current sales
- Total sales from inactive customers
- Total sales from prospectives
- Total sales from active customers

---

## 10. Old Customer Details
Display:
- Customer name
- Terms
- Last purchase date
- Status (active/inactive)
- Balance
- Other relevant historical info

---
