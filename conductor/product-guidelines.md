# Product Guidelines

## Tone and Prose
The application should maintain a **friendly and approachable** tone. Documentation and the user interface should be engaging and helpful, making complex management tasks feel accessible and less daunting for all users.

## Visual Identity & UX
The UI should adhere to **clean and minimalist** design principles. It must prioritize whitespace and simplicity to reduce cognitive load, allowing users to focus on their core tasks without being overwhelmed by excessive visual noise.

## User Support & Onboarding
User success is paramount. The application will feature a **comprehensive interactive tour and tooltips** to guide users through the various dashboards and tools, ensuring they can leverage the full power of the platform from day one.
