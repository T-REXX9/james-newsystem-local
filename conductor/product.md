# Product Guide

## Project Goal
The primary goal of this application is to provide a comprehensive Business Management Dashboard combined with a specialized Sales Performance & Leaderboard tracking system. It aims to centralize sales data, customer information, inventory management, and staff oversight into a unified, AI-enhanced platform.

## Primary Users
- **Management/Owners:** For high-level metrics, strategic oversight, and staff management.
- **Sales Agents:** For tracking individual performance, managing leads, and monitoring leaderboards.
- **Staff/Operations:** For executing daily transactions, maintaining inventory, and handling operational tasks.

## Key Features
- **Sales Dashboard & Real-time Leaderboards:** Visualizing sales performance and fostering healthy competition among agents.
- **Customer Relationship Management (CRM) & Contact Database:** Managing customer interactions and maintaining a centralized contact repository.
- **Inventory Management & Order Processing:** Streamlining the order lifecycle and tracking stock levels.
- **Role-Based Access Control (RBAC) & User Management:** Ensuring secure access to sensitive data based on user roles and permissions.
